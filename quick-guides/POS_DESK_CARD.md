# 🖥️ POS DESK CARD — Quick Sale Steps

> **Post at every cashier workstation**

---

## ✅ QUICK SALE STEPS

```
SCAN → VERIFY → ADD
PRICE CHECK (if no barcode: search by name)
POM? → REQUEST PRESCRIPTION
PAYMENT: M-PESA STK / CASH / CARD / INSURANCE
PRINT / SMS RECEIPT
HANDOVER WITH LABEL
```

---

## 🚩 RED FLAGS — STOP & ESCALATE

| Flag | Action |
|------|--------|
| No expiry date visible | ❌ REJECT |
| Damaged packaging | 🔶 QUARANTINE |
| Prescription >6 months old | ❌ REJECT |
| Missing pharmacist signature | ❌ DO NOT DISPENSE |
